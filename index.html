What's going on?

It's been a long time since this site has been under active development. It's taken a global pandemic to remind my just how much I enjoy building thing with code.

I've taken the opportunity to start developing with Tailwind, although I prefer the elegance of semantic classnames, I understand the attraction of more flexible classnames and wanted to test drive the CSS framework.

Tailwind also helps balance my limitations with designs by providing some great examples, so there's that as well!
